We are leader in
MITRC since 2007

MITRC Engineering College in Alwar is one of the best engineering colleges in Alwar. MITRC offers various programs, including UG & PG in Artificial Intelligence and its specialization, Computer Science Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering. MITRC has established itself in 2007 and proved itself as a leader in technical education, making it ranked among the top 10 engineering colleges in Alwar. Along with other engineering institutes in Alwar like Institute of Engineering and Technology Alwar, Laxmi Devi Institute of Engineering and Technology Alwar, Sunrise University, and NIET Alwar, MITRC has consistently produced successful graduates who have excelled in various fields and met the demand of society and industries. Thus, our alumni success makes MITRC the top B.Tech private college in Alwar. The college is known for its impressive placements and has seen students securing positions in top companies. MITRC is affiliated with Bikaner Technical University and Rajasthan Technical University (Old Batches). MITRC ensures that its students are well-prepared for the industry as well as for society. The college has been ranked as one of the top engineering colleges in Alwar and boasts a high cut-off for admissions, especially in Computer Science Engineering and its Specialization.
